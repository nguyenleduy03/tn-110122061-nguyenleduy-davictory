## Official IELTS Speaking Band Descriptors (use as strict reference)

### Fluency and Coherence (FC)
- **Band 9**: Speaks fluently with only rare repetition/self-correction; hesitation is content-related. Uses cohesive features appropriately.
- **Band 8**: Speaks fluently with occasional repetition/self-correction; uses a range of cohesive features flexibly.
- **Band 7**: Speaks at length without noticeable effort; uses a range of connectives and discourse markers with some flexibility.
- **Band 6**: Willing to speak at length, though may lose coherence at times. Uses a range of connectives but not always appropriately.
- **Band 5**: Usually maintains flow but uses repetition/self-correction. Uses basic connectives repetitively.

### Lexical Resource (LR)
- **Band 9**: Uses vocabulary with full flexibility and precision; idiomatic language naturally and accurately.
- **Band 8**: Wide vocabulary resource readily and flexibly; idiomatic language skillfully.
- **Band 7**: Uses vocabulary flexibly to discuss a variety of topics; some less common and idiomatic vocabulary.
- **Band 6**: Enough vocabulary to discuss topics at length; attempts paraphrase but sometimes inappropriate.
- **Band 5**: Limited flexibility; attempts paraphrase with some success.

### Grammatical Range and Accuracy (GRA)
- **Band 9**: Full range of structures naturally and appropriately; consistently accurate.
- **Band 8**: Wide range of structures flexibly; most sentences error-free.
- **Band 7**: Range of complex structures with some flexibility; frequently error-free sentences.
- **Band 6**: Mix of simple and complex structures but limited flexibility; errors in complex structures rarely cause comprehension problems.
- **Band 5**: Limited range of structures; complex structures attempted but errors may cause comprehension problems.

### Pronunciation (P)
- **Band 9**: Full range of pronunciation features with precision and subtlety.
- **Band 8**: Wide range of pronunciation features; sustains flexible use with occasional lapses.
- **Band 7**: Range of pronunciation features with mixed control; some sustained use.
- **Band 6**: Range of pronunciation features with some control; effective but inconsistent.
- **Band 5**: Limited range of pronunciation features; L1 influence evident.
